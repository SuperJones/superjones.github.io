Save the Scimitar Oryx

A web application that is an expansion of the Source Population Alliance, who are doing research on and helping to bring Scimitar Oryx (a species that is going extinct) back to the wild. This database will be used by ranches and other institutions. They have populations of the oryx and need help keeping track of things like oryx numbers, gender, location, breeding history (can't let them get inbred), and so on. Once a stable gene pool and other information can be established, researchers are hoping to re-introduce them into the wild!

Viva la Vegan

A web application for the modern vegan. This app is designed to help vegans find the vegan food options at fast food chains and restaurants. Instead of Googling "what can I eat at this restaurant?," and searching through websites and blogs for the answer, you can now easily find everything in one app. It has full CRUD functionality, built with AngularJS on top of a Ruby on Rails API.
